About

    Welcome, this website was created to provide useful tools and information regarding the process of making and enjoying third-wave specialty coffee. Third-wave coffee is a movement that started in the early 2000's which promotes high-quality coffee production and connoisseurship. Specialty coffee aims to emphasize the unique qualities and characteristics coffee beans might have due to their diverse genetic nature. As a result, many methods of consumption and production have been created along the way.

Why Manual?

    Many people prefer manual drip coffee for the amount of control it allows them over the brewing process. The complexity of coffee means that every detail counts. Some of the details that can affect the flavors & aromas of the brew include:

  • Coffee Variety
  • Bean Processing
  • Roasting Method
  • Roast Profile
  • Grind Size & Evenness
  • Brew Method
  • Water Temperature

Each of theses factors contribute to the subtle flavors we experience when tasting coffee. Besides the wonderful flavor & aroma, manual brews can also be fun to make. It's exciting to see the small changes you make to the coffee manifest in tangible ways.

Final Thoughts...

    The process of making coffee can be intriguing to some and excessive to others. Most people enjoy coffee for its caffeine or the warmth it brings in the mornings. Whether you consider yourself to be a producer, connoisseur, or just someone that needs a jolt of caffeine; coffee is there to fill that niche.
