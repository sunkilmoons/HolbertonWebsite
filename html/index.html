About

Welcome, this website was created to provide useful tools and information regarding the process of making and enjoying third wave specialty coffee. Third wave coffee is a movement that started in the early 2000's that promotes high-quality coffee production and connoisseurship.
    Specialty coffee aims to emphasize the unique qualities and characteristics coffee beans might have due to their diverse genetic nature. Many methods of consumption and production have been created along the way to highlight different qualities of the beans.
    Below is a tool you can use to help you with your manual pour overs and immersion methods. It is important to be consistent if you would like to produce great tasting coffee everytime. Select the brewer you have and be sure to follow along with the steps provided. Enjoy!
