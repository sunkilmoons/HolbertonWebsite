The Roast

    Roasting perhaps plays the largest role in the flavor of coffee. The roasting process is all about breaking down the organic compounds within the seed. This is done through manipulation of thermal & kinetic energy. Coffee roasting is a destructive process, so it's important for the roaster to find a balance between how much and when they would like to introduce that energy.

    In the coffee industry, roasting is considered to be a meld between science and art. There is never a correct way to roast because taste is subjective and every bean is different. The science of roasting is important for professionals in order to create a consistent product. Changes in temperature are usually monitored throughout the roaster as well as airflow. Graphs are usually made in real time, depicting delta temperatures and control changes.
